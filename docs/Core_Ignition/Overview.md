##Overview

Core Ignition is a crafted six-month incentive program designed to reward the Core community for their valuable contributions to the network. By participating, users can not only earn rewards but also play a crucial role in fostering the growth and adoption of the Core ecosystem. Through Core Ignition, you have the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to the evolving BTCfi landscape while being rewarded for your efforts. Together, we're building a more accessible and robust BTCfi ecosystem for all.
